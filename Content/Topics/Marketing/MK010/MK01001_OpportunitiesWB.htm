Workbench

Outcome Reason and Outcome Comment fields have been added to are found in the Workbench > Details page. They only appear, based on setup, when the Stage field is set to a Closed-Lost, Inactive, or Closed-Won value (see Without Outcome Reason/Comment Fields and With Outcome Reason/Comment Fields). The Outcome Reason field is populated from Outcome Reason Field maintenance located in > Setup > Setup.

Both of these fields are available in Column Sets and Bulk Add/Update. The Outcome Reason field is also available in Multi-update.
